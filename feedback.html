Ocean Programmes & Opportunities

Explore volunteering, campaigns, and initiatives you can join to make a real difference for our oceans and marine ecosystems.

Programme Listings

Ocean Conservancy Clean-Up Drive

Join the world's largest ocean clean-up effort. Volunteer locally or participate in the International Coastal Cleanup to remove marine debris before it enters the sea.

Marine Protected Area Monitoring

Assist researchers and conservation agencies in monitoring ocean biodiversity inside MPAs. Suitable for students with an interest in marine biology or ecology.

Mangrove Restoration Project

Take part in community-led mangrove replanting along Sri Lanka's coastal belt. Mangroves protect coastlines, store carbon, and provide vital habitats for juvenile fish.

SDG Youth Advocacy Programme

Develop your voice as a young advocate for ocean policy. Attend workshops, produce campaigns, and present recommendations to local government and community groups.

Citizen Science: Ocean Data Collection

Contribute to global ocean health databases by recording local observations water temperature, plastic sightings, and marine species through guided mobile apps.
