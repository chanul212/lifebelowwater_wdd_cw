Explore the World Beneath the Waves!

The ocean is more than water - it is the foundation of all life on Earth. From the coral reefs of the tropics to the dark depths of the ocean floor, marine ecosystems support billions of people, regulate our climate, and harbour some of the most extraordinary biodiversity on the planet. Yet human activity is pushing these systems to their limits. Browse through our gallery to discover the key issues, wonders, and solutions connected to Life Below Water and find out what each of us can do to make a difference.
