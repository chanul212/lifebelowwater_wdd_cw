This page provides W3C HTML validation evidence for all pages implemented by Pabhasith Ranathunga. All pages were validated at validator.w3.org with no errors. Screenshots from the W3C validator are included below for each page.

Reflection on Validation

The W3C validation process helped identify structural issues that were not visible during visual browser testing. During initial validation of quiz.html, a missing alt attribute on one action card image was flagged and corrected. In splash.html, a redundant type="text/javascript" attribute was noted as an informational warning (not an error) and removed as a matter of best practice.

Running validation after each significant change -rather than only at the end -made it easier to identify the exact cause of each issue. All pages in my submission produced no validation errors in their final submitted state.
